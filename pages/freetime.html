Music

When I was little, I would just randomly sing, all the time. And I was good at it. For some reason I stopped singing after 6th grade. Luckily, my passion for music was restored a couple of years back, and now my neighbours can hear me singing nearly every day.

I play the piano and the guitar, and I sing. I'm the type of guy that doesn't just go around learning all the popular songs, to show off. I learn the songs that I love, and they are usually some people haven't heard about. Try Six - Sleeping At Last.

I also create my own music. It allows me to be creative, and I've found out that I'm the most happy when I'm creative. I have written about 20 songs, on both the piano and the guitar, 10 of which I really, really like. You can hear some of them on my website - Creativity Corner.
