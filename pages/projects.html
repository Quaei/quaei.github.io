The Uno Game

https://sakamuno.herokuapp.com

(Disclaimer: please give it a little time to load, I am using a free-hosting service, and it takes a while sometimes.)


It was a summer evening when I first got the idea to do something bigger, a project on a scale I have never done before. Hence, the Uno was born. Well... not so fast. Let me tell you its story.

As I said, it was summer, everyone's free, we were going out literally every day/night. This was back in 2018. From the day I got the idea, and started coding the Uno, every day I got more and more captivated by it. I started coming late to the hangouts we had, to the coffees I and my friends went to, and to everything else.

Fast forward one month, here we have it, Uno 1.0; My friends and I got so into it, we played it for hours and hours. Of course, it was full of bugs, which I was glad to fix, one by one.

Now we have Uno 2.0; So, more about the project: It's the uno card game, but modernized and digitalized. It has a bunch of cool game modes, cool features like a soundtrack, playing with bots, etc.

It has a full intuitive design, it has ranks to keep track of your wins/losses, and it's REALLY fun! I'll let you see it for yourself, by clicking on the link above.

Creativity Corner

https://creativitycorner.herokuapp.com

(Disclaimer: please give it a little time to load, I am using a free-hosting service, and it takes a while sometimes.)


Driven by the success of the Uno website, I soon came up with the idea for another website - A Creativity Corner - To which people can come and listen to wonderful music.

This coincided with my passion for creating music, so it was a win-win, and I immediately got to work.

With this project I also explored concepts about js and nodejs -programming that I hadn't touched up on before. Like the concept of video chatting, private chatting, embed websites, etc.

Recently I changed it up a bit, and now we have Creativity Corner 2.0; I love it because it has a much more simplistic design, and focuses on what it really is about - Music - and not a bunch of other things like for example the video chatting I had before.

Again, I won't hold you any longer, you can check it out by clicking on the link above.
